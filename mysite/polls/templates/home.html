About Me

I'm an advocate of impact investing, triple-bottom-line business, and entrepreneurship more generally.

For 3 years I worked as a consultant providing quantitative and qualitative analysis on climate policy, renewable energy, and the carbon market.

I've always been interested in clothing and textiles and in 2011 I started programming and co-founded Clothesliner, a website that described the fit of clothing by comparing any item to something you're familiar with.

I currently work as an independent consultant in the carbon market / impact investing space and am always looking to meet other entrepreneurs, social or otherwise. Email me at ptrklly (at gmail).
