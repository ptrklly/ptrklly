About Me

I'm an advocate of impact investing, triple-bottom-line business, and entrepreneurship more generally.

For 3 years I worked as a consultant providing quantitative and qualitative analysis on climate and energy policy and the carbon market.

I've always been interested in clothing and textiles and in 2011 I started programming and cofounded Clothesliner, a website that described the fit of clothing by comparing any item to something you're familiar with.

I'm currently an independent consultant working in the carbon market and always looking to meet other entrepreneurs, social or otherwise. Email me at ptrklly (at gmail).
